About Captain Sam Quint

Sam Quint is a grizzled, seasoned shark hunter, who resides on the Island of Amity, MA in the shanty seaside port of Menemsha. He runs a bootleg distillery, and a whale oil business, as well as a weekend charter service. He captains the Orca.

He also served aboard the USS Indianapolis.

The Orca

The Orca was designed to seek out and catch sharks. It includes an expanded fly bridge, surplus search light, and a reinforced crow's nest to aid in measuring dorsal fins from extended distances. Captain Quint is steadfastly against using fancy or advanced hardware, or portable cages to capture or kill sharks, he does employ a limited amount of rudimentary tools. Many of which are homemade.
